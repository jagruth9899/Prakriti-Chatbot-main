About Us

Prakruti is a fundamental concept in Indian philosophy and Ayurvedic medicine, representing the innate, natural constitution and balance of an individual's physical and mental characteristics. It is believed to be determined at the time of conception and remains largely constant throughout a person's life. Prakruti is a unique combination of the three doshas—Vata, Pitta, and Kapha—which are dynamic energies governing various bodily functions and traits. Understanding one's prakruti is crucial in Ayurveda, as it guides personalized health and wellness practices, including diet, lifestyle, and therapeutic interventions, to maintain harmony and prevent imbalances that may lead to illness. By recognizing and harmonizing with their prakruti, individuals can optimize their overall well-being and vitality.
